About Honey Tales Africa

Carol Honey

Author & Poet

Carol Honey is the independent self-published author of the Honey Tales Africa series of children's books. She has been writing humorous poems for her family and friends her whole life, but it was when she started writing for her Grand-Children that her friends and family persuaded her to publish.

Carol has always had a keen interest in the wildlife of South Africa and wanted to teach her Grand-Children about conservation and the love of all animals and nature.

She is retired and lives and works out of her home in Centurion, and is spending her time working on the next book in the series. With the assistance of her husband David, Carol sells her books at various craft markets and direct to schools.
