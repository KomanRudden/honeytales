The Author

Carol Honey

Poet & Author of the Honey Tales Africa Series

Carol has been writing humorous poems for her family and friends her whole life — but it was the arrival of her grandchildren that inspired her to share them with the world.

A Lifelong Love of Words and Wildlife

Carol Honey is the independent self-published author of the Honey Tales Africa series of children's books. She has been writing humorous poems for her family and friends her whole life, but it was when she started writing for her grandchildren that her friends and family persuaded her to publish.

Carol has always had a keen interest in the wildlife of South Africa and wanted to teach her grandchildren about conservation and the love of all animals and nature. The poems are crafted to delight young readers while quietly nurturing a respect and wonder for the natural world they share.

She is retired and lives and works out of her home in Centurion, spending her time working on the next book in the series. With the assistance of her husband David, Carol sells her books at various craft markets and direct to schools across South Africa.
